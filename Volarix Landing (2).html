Straight answers.

Volarix is a trading journal in build. The thesis: most journals show you raw data and stop there. Volarix is being built to do the next step — find the specific behavioral pattern that's costing you money, name it, quantify the dollar impact, and tell you what to change. Nothing more. Nothing magical.

Closed beta opens 2026. Founding cohort first. No firm public launch date yet — we'd rather ship it when it's actually useful than when the calendar says so. Early access members get every build as it lands.

ChatGPT has never seen your trades. Even if you paste them in, it can't run statistical analysis across hundreds of them. Volarix is a focused tool, not a chatbot: it ingests your full history, runs the same set of behavioral checks across every account, and writes the report. The output is a number, a pattern, a recommendation — not a conversation.

Honest answer for pre-launch: right now we're not collecting any trade data — there's nothing to upload yet. Before founding members onboard, we'll publish the full data-handling spec: where it's stored, who can access it, how you delete it. Anything we promise will be written down and verifiable.
